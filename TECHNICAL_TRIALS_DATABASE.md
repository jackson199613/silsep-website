# Silsep Technical Trials Database (The 38 Documents)

This database summarizes the core technical findings from Silsep's 38 internal trial documents, integrated into the sales and technical workflows for the Voice AI (Dialo).

## 1. AquaScience™ Solutions (Aquaculture)

### AquaConvertPlus™ (Molecular Shielding) - [Trial #22/38]
*   **Technology**: Synthetic silicate carrier with an open-network molecular architecture (Molecular Shielding).
*   **Challenge**: Degradation of exogenous enzymes during high-temperature (95°C) steam conditioning in tropical feed mills.
*   **Performance**:
    *   **Enzyme Recovery**: 88% recovery post-pelleting (vs. 40% for standard carriers).
    *   **FCR (Mekong Delta)**: 1.45 FCR (vs. 1.62 control) - a reduction of 0.17.
    *   **Growth**: +13.7% Average Body Weight Gain (30 days).
*   **Value Prop**: Prevents "Nutritional Leak" and maximizes enzyme bioavailability.

### AquaPure™ (Water Rescue) - [Trial #31/38]
*   **Technology**: Mesoporous silicate structure ("Molecular Sponge").
*   **Challenge**: Sudden ammonia spikes in shrimp/tilapia ponds.
*   **Performance**:
    *   **Ammonia Adsorption**: Dropped ammonia levels by 1.5 ppm within 4 hours.
    *   **Survival Rate**: 90% survival recovery during emergency spikes.
*   **Value Prop**: Immediate rescue of pond water quality without slow water exchange.

## 2. Animal Nutrition & Health (Swine & Poultry)

### ToxinShut™ (Mycotoxin Deactivation) - [Trial #12/38]
*   **Technology**: STS (Specific Toxin Shielding) - Enzymatic cleavage of Zen molecules.
*   **Challenge**: High Zearalenone (ZEN) levels in corn causing reproductive failure in breeding stock.
*   **Performance**:
    *   **ZEN Deactivation**: 95% deactivation rate (not just binding).
    *   **Reproductive Success**: 15% increase in litter size; zero toxin-related failure over two cycles.
*   **Value Prop**: Active deactivation of toxins rather than passive binding.

### GutPlus™ + ImmunoGuard™ (Gut Integrity) - [Trial #05/38]
*   **Technology**: Tight junction reinforcement + immune pathway activation.
*   **Challenge**: High mortality in AGP-free poultry production due to necrotic enteritis.
*   **Performance**:
    *   **Mortality**: 40% reduction in mortality rates.
    *   **Gut Health**: 66% reduction in intestinal lesion scores; FCR improved by 0.06.
*   **Value Prop**: Sustainable AGP-free growth with superior gut health.
