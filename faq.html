Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers for R&D and Quality Control professionals.

All technical data and performance benchmarks are verified through Internal Method #22-A at the UCSD Colloid Science Lab.

What is SILSEP?

A specialized range of silica carriers designed for the modern premix industry, born from UCSD colloid science.

How is it different from standard silica?

Standard silica is general-purpose; SILSEP is engineered for specific premix variables like static control, uniformity, and moisture management.

Why is my CV value always high?

Likely due to particle size mismatch or static build-up. SILSEP's narrow D50 (30-50µm) matches vitamin crystals, solving segregation issues. Learn more about SP-Premix →

Zeolite is cheaper, why switch?

Zeolite costs more in 'Quality Loss' due to toxins, high moisture, and high CV. SILSEP pays for itself through FCR improvement and lower downtime. Calculate your ROI →

What about Rice Hull?

Rice hull has inconsistent density and high mycotoxin risks. SILSEP is a stable, inorganic, and toxin-free alternative.

What is the inclusion rate?

Typically 0.5% to 2% depending on the specific application and target uniformity.

Does it dilute the formula?

No, its high efficiency often allows for lower total carrier inclusion than traditional clay-based carriers.

What is the MOQ?

Standard MOQ is 1 metric ton (40 bags of 25kg each).

Can you export to Vietnam/India?

Yes, we have a global logistics network with specific focus on SE Asia and the Americas.

What is the significance of ≤6% moisture?

Lower moisture prevents the degradation of heat-sensitive vitamins and enzymes during storage and processing.

Does SILSEP interfere with vitamin activity?

No, it is chemically inert and protects vitamins even during 95°C steam pelleting.

Do you have FAMI-QS?

We are ISO 9001 certified; FAMI-QS application is in progress, expected by Q4 2026.

Submit the request form on our website; we ship technical samples globally within 48 hours.

How do I use SP-Carrier for Choline Chloride?

It can absorb up to 2.5x its weight. We provide specific high-loading protocols for liquid absorption. View SP-Carrier Specs →

Can SP-XF solve static in medicated feed?

Yes, it reduces static potential by over 40% vs. standard carriers, ensuring dosage precision. View SP-XF Specs →

Is SILSEP safe for aquaculture?

Yes, it is a high-purity inorganic material with zero organic toxins or heavy metal risks.

What is D50?

The median particle diameter. Matching D50 is the key to preventing premix segregation.

Why 30-50 microns?

This range matches the average crystal size of most vitamins and mineral salts for geometric dilution.

How does it help with shelf life?

By acting as a moisture sink and providing a chemically inert buffer between active ingredients.

Can I use it for liquid enzymes?

Yes, SP-Carrier is designed specifically for stable, high-loading liquid-to-powder conversion.
