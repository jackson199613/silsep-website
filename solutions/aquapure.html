Q: What makes AquaPure™ the ideal premix carrier silica?

A: As a specialized **premix carrier silica** and **feed flow agent**, AquaPure™ features a D50 of 30-50 microns, ensuring perfect homogeneity in feed mixing. Its mesoporous structure makes it the premier **anti-caking agent for feed premix**, especially for high-loading applications like a **carrier for choline chloride 50% powder** or a **free flow agent for vitamin premix**.

A: AquaPure™ is engineered as a **low moisture carrier for trace minerals**, preventing oxidation and maintaining stability in extreme conditions.
