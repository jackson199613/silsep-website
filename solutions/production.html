The Challenge: High-Fat Aqua-Feed Extrusion

Industrial aqua-feed production, particularly for shrimp and high-energy fish species, often involves high inclusion rates of oils and heat-sensitive additives. This presents severe challenges: bridging in silos, caking during storage, and massive nutrient loss during high-temperature pelleting/extrusion (up to 95°C).

Critical Pain Points:

  • Bridging and poor flow in bulk handling
  • Heat degradation of vitamins and enzymes
  • Poor inclusion uniformity of liquid micro-ingredients

The Science of Carriers

We provide the world's most advanced molecular silica carriers, engineered specifically for the feed industry's unique demands. Our carriers turn unstable liquids into high-potency, free-flowing powders.

Superior Flowability

Advanced silica morphology eliminates inter-particle friction, preventing bridging and ensuring consistent inclusion rates at the mixer.

Thermal Shield

Porous structure acts as a thermal buffer, protecting heat-sensitive enzymes (like Phytase) from the intense temperatures of the conditioning chamber.

High Loading Capacity

With oil absorption rates exceeding 300%, our carriers allow for the concentration of liquid actives into compact, dry premixes.
