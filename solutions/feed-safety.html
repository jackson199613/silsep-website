The Challenge: Multi-Toxin Contamination

Tropical aquaculture regions (Vietnam, SE Asia) face extreme high temperatures and humidity, accelerating the growth of *Aspergillus* and *Fusarium* molds. This leads to multi-mycotoxin contamination (Aflatoxins, T2, Fumonisins, DON) that can cause liver damage, immunosuppression, and massive mortality in aquatic species.

Critical Pain Points:

  • Mould growth in stored raw materials
  • Sub-lethal mycotoxin levels impacting growth
  • Oxidative stress and rancidity in high-fat aquafeeds

Multi-Action Feed Protection

We provide a comprehensive barrier against biological and chemical threats. Our ToxinShut™ platform represents the state-of-the-art in molecular toxin deactivation.

Irreversible Adsorption

Synthetic Tetrahedral Silicates (STS) provide massive surface area for irreversible binding of polar toxins like Aflatoxin B1.

Enzymatic Deactivation

Targeting non-polar toxins (Zearalenone, Fumonisin) that cannot be bound, our enzymes degrade them into non-toxic metabolites in vivo.

Gas-Phase Preservation

Buffered organic acid vapors penetrate feed voids to eliminate mould colonies where traditional powders cannot reach.
