Zeolite: The Moisture Magnet

Natural zeolite is highly porous and hydrophilic. It frequently arrives with moisture levels exceeding 12%. In tropical regions, this water content reacts with heat-sensitive vitamins, drastically reducing their potency before they even reach the farm.

Rice Hull: The Mycotoxin Variable

Agricultural by-products like rice hulls are prone to mycotoxin contamination and heavy metal absorption. For high-value aquaculture species (shrimp, salmon), these contaminants can lead to increased mortality and poor FCR.

SILSEP Silica: Engineered for Zero Loss
