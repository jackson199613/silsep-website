Why SILSEP

The Science Behind Every Bag

Every product in our range is built on three pillars: proven field data, transparent ingredient science, and cost-in-use leadership.

IAS Patent Technology

Proprietary Integrated Atomisation & Spray-drying (IAS) — produces carrier-free, 100% water-soluble micro-spheres with uniform particle size and heat stability through pelleting.

Field-Validated Results

AromaBoost™ 2312: +53 g/day piglet feed intake, palatability index 1.792 vs 1.472 for top import brand. PalateX™ X382: +13.65% daily feed intake, +10.58% ADG in 30-day trial.

Transparent Ingredients

All active ingredients listed in the National Feed Additive Directory. OxiGuard™ W5 carries Class A(1) safety classification. No hidden carriers, no mystery blends.

Cost-In-Use Leadership

SweetFlow™ at 150 g/ton delivers uniform sweetness to every pellet. SweetCore™ at 60–180 g/ton costs less than alternatives at 3× the dose. EmulsiMax™ enables energy matrix reduction that pays for itself.
