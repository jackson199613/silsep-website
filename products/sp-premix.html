SP-Premix

The Standard-Setting Carrier for Vitamin & Trace Mineral Premixes

Technical Profile

SP-Premix is a molecularly engineered silica carrier designed for high-precision animal nutrition. By controlling the surface charge and particle size distribution (D50 35-45µm), it ensures that micro-ingredients remain uniformly distributed throughout the mix, preventing segregation and static-induced loss. For a deeper look at mixing physics, read our article on Reducing CV Values →

  • D50 Particle Size35-45 µm
  • Oil Absorption160-180 g/100g
