## 2. Jackson Jiang's First-Touch Protocol
Mandatory restrictions for the first email (before the first reply):

*   **Forbidden Content**:
    *   ❌ NO Website links.
    *   ❌ NO Images.
    *   ❌ NO Prices.
    *   ❌ NO Videos.
    *   ❌ NO "Manufacturer" (use "Provider" instead).
    *   ❌ NO "Supply" (use "Provide" instead).
*   **Structure**: Keep it extremely short and concise.
*   **Targeting**:
    *   Avoid generic emails (`info@`, `contact@`).
    *   Prioritize specific **Procurement/Technical contacts**.
*   **Personalization**: Always analyze the prospect's product line and customize the matching logic before sending.

## 3. Language Rule
*   Always send the email in the **native language** of the target country whenever possible (e.g., Spanish for Mexico, local language for Europe).
