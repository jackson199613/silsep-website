Know Before You Buy

Frequently Asked Questions

What feed sweeteners does SILSEP offer? +

SILSEP offers two sweetener products: SweetFlow™ 7188 — an IAS patent spray-dried sweetener that is 100% water-soluble in 3–5 seconds at 150–300 g/ton; and SweetCore™ T3965 — a neotame and saccharin sodium compound on silica carrier at 60–180 g/ton. Both are pellet-stable and provide sucrose-like palatability.

What is the dosage for AromaBoost™ 2312 milk flavour? +

AromaBoost™ 2312 is recommended at 400–600 g/ton for pelleted complete feed (up to 800 g/ton for stronger aroma). For concentrate and premix: 2.0–3.0 kg/ton. Specifically designed for nursery piglets and creep feed — trial data: +53 g/day feed intake vs control, palatability index 1.792 vs 1.000 (control).

How much fish meal does AromaBoost™ 1437 replace? +

AromaBoost™ 1437 at 400–600 g/ton delivers the palatability equivalent of 30 kg/ton fish meal aroma. For formulas with 2–3% fish meal: 800–1,000 g/ton. For fish meal-free formulas: 1.0–1.5 kg/ton. Aroma retention proven for 90 days in finished feed.

What trial results does PalateX™ X382 have? +

A 30-day field trial with 50-day weaned piglets at 600 g/ton showed: +13.65% daily feed intake, +10.58% average daily gain, and 3.53% FCR improvement. PalateX™ X382 combines natural cream enzymolysate, MCFA/SCFA, lactone esters, and ethyl vanillin for a fresh milk-aroma profile.

What is PalateX™ X383 and how does it differ from X382? +

PalateX™ X383 is an all-in-one compound attractant combining plant essential oils (anise oil + wintergreen oil), neotame, saccharin sodium, and feed-grade flavour. Unlike X382 (pure milk-aroma attractant with MCFA), X383 replaces three separate additives in one bag and adds gut health benefits from essential oils. Dosage: 200–500 g/ton; delivers +10–20% feed intake and slaughter 10–15 days earlier.

Is OxiGuard™ W5 safe for continuous long-term use? +

Yes. All active ingredients in OxiGuard™ W5 (TBHQ + BHT + chelator) are listed in the Chinese National Feed Additive Directory and classified as Class A(1) — the highest safety tier. No bitter or astringent aftertaste. Safe at 100–300 g/ton for complete feed and up to 3,000 g/ton for fish meal and meat-bone meal preservation.

How does EmulsiMax™ reduce feed cost? +

EmulsiMax™ uses lysophosphatidylcholine (LPC) to improve fat micellisation and intestinal lipid absorption. This allows formulation of an energy matrix reduction — reducing oil inclusion by an equivalent energy value while maintaining performance. The cost saving on oil typically exceeds the cost of EmulsiMax™ at the recommended 100–300 g/ton dosage.
