The SILSEP Story

SILSEP was founded as a specialized division of Sensil to address a critical gap in the feed premix industry: the lack of precision carriers. While nutritionists focused on active ingredients, the carriers remained primitive, leading to high CV values, static clogging, and shelf-life instability.

By leveraging advanced surface modification techniques, SILSEP transformed silica from a simple flow agent into a precision-engineered carrier system capable of delivering pharmaceutical-grade uniformity to industrial feed production.

Our Manufacturing Power

Located in Sanming, Fujian, our production base operates at the intersection of scale and precision. Utilizing proprietary surface-coating reactors, we produce over 100,000 tons of engineered silica annually, serving global feed giants with batch-to-batch consistency that sets the industry benchmark.
